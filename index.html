What is the perimeter of parallelogram?
The perimeter of a parallelogram is the distance around the parallelogram.
How to calculate perimeter of parallelogram?
To calculate perimeter of parallelogram you should use the following formula:
perimeter of parallelogram = 2 * (width of the parallelogram + length of the parallelogram)
